Install on iPhone/iPad
  1. Host this folder on any HTTPS website.
  2. Open the site in Safari.
  3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open from the Home Screen and allow microphone access.

Note: iOS may pause microphone/audio when the screen locks or Safari/Home Screen app goes fully into the background.
